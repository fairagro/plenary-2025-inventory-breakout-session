Suggest a Repository to re3data.org

A simplified guide to the submission process

Submit the Application

Operators or owners of a repository fill out the online application form on the re3data.org website. This is the first step to get the infrastructure considered for the registry.

1

Editorial Analysis

The re3data.org team thoroughly analyzes the repository's website based on a detailed handbook. They check for key information such as data access, metadata standards, and terms of use.

2

Meet Minimum Requirements

For a repository to be indexed, it must meet specific requirements. This includes clear explanations of data access and terms of use, as well as a focus on research data.

3

Review and Publication

A second team member reviews all the gathered information to ensure accuracy. Once approved, the new repository record is published and becomes visible to the public in the re3data.org registry.
