Why Do Global Registries Matter?

To make agricultural research data truly FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable), we need to ensure our data repositories are visible to a global audience. Global registries act as trusted, central directories. They are the essential first step in putting FAIRagro data on the world map, allowing researchers, institutions, and automated tools to discover and connect with our valuable resources. This guide explores the two key platforms that form the foundation of this strategy.
